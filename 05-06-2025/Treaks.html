Kedarnath Treak

🌄 1. Kedarkantha Trek – "The Queen of Snow Treks"
📍 Location: Sankri Village, Uttarakhand
🕒 Best Time: December to April
⛰ Altitude: ~12,500 ft
🥾 Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
📅 Duration: 4–6 Days
🌟 Highlights: Snow trails, pine forests, Juda-ka-Talab, summit views of 13 Himalayan peaks
📝 Description: Kedarkantha is one of the most loved winter treks in India. It’s perfect for beginners and offers a fairytale experience — snow-covered pine forests, frozen lakes, and beautiful sunrise views from the summit. The trail starts from Sankri village and takes you through remote hamlets and quiet mountain woods. On reaching the peak, you’ll witness views of Swargarohini, Bandarpoonch, and Black Peak ranges.
